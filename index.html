wolves

that live and hunt in packs,
typically led by an alpha pair.
They are highly intelligent and
adaptable, with a wide range of habitats
across the globe. Wolves play a crucial role
in maintaining ecosystem balance by controlling
prey populations. Their hunting strategies and social
structures are fascinating aspects of their biology.
